The Mantra Process

Emotional Modifier: 'Creative'

I want my clients to realize that the quality of work they are recieving is the utmost creative and unique, tailored specifically to their needs.

Descriptive Modifier: 'Digital'

The nature of offering I provide my clients is purely digital. Brands must leverage digital platforms to maintain a competitive edge. I am that edge.

Brand Functions: 'Developer'

I am a developer. It's what I love to do - from web and app design to photography and videography, I love developing creative, quality, content.

Put it All Together:

'Creative Digial Developer'

I had a lot of words on my list, but I believe my mantra best describes what I love to do. I love challenging myself by constantly developing creative content.

The Pitch Process

Using the guide Professor Ela posted, these were the steps I followed to draft my perfect pitch!

What are my Passions?

I asked myself, why do I do web design? I have an insane passion for art and, it may be cliche, but I love helping others. I put these two together and created my profession!

What do I do Best?

My greatest strenght in this area is my time management and my ability to never over-commit. I understand that a client's time is valuble, so I want to be able to execute the highest quality of work, while maintaining a personable relationship.

What do I want to Achieve?

I want to keep learning and expand my knowledge in every area of digital design. I love every second of it, and I can only get better by learning from my mistakes!
